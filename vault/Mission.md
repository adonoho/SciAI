# Mission

## Statement

SciAI exists to explore and implement an **AI-augmented scientific researcher** — a system that extends human scientific capability through AI collaboration rather than replacing it.

## Founders

- [[People/Andrew Donoho|Andrew Donoho]]
- [[People/Andy Terrel|Andy Terrel]]

## Phased Scope

### Phase 1 — Claude-First
Build the initial augmented researcher using Claude as the primary AI agent. Understand what works, what falls short, and what patterns generalize.

### Phase 2 — Multi-Agent / Multi-System
Extend to other agentic AI systems. Evaluate how different systems compare on scientific research tasks.

## Core Questions

- What does a working AI-augmented research workflow look like end-to-end?
- Which research tasks benefit most from AI augmentation?
- How should a human researcher and an AI agent divide cognitive labor?
- What evaluation criteria distinguish useful augmentation from noise?
